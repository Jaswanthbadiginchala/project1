Spicy Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 500g shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and chili flakes; sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp and cook for 3–4 minutes until pink.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
